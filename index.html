Automated Supply Chain ETL Pipeline & Data Quality Control Tower

April 2026

In enterprise logistics, messy data isn't just an IT problem—it's a massive financial liability. I built the Inventory Control Tower to solve the 'Data Quality Gap' between raw warehouse logs and executive balance sheets.

This project simulates a high-volume supply chain environment where data is fraught with human entry errors and missing logistics codes. Using Mage AI, I orchestrated a robust ETL pipeline that sweeps incoming data batches, applies statistical anomaly detection (Tukey’s Fences) to catch pricing outliers, and loads the clean data into a high-performance DuckDB instance. The architecture ensures strict idempotency, meaning the pipeline can run continuously without duplicating records.

The backend powers a custom Streamlit Business Intelligence dashboard. Rather than just displaying data, the dashboard acts as a strategic operational tool—translating raw anomalies into a 'Capital at Risk' financial metric and allowing managers to isolate exactly where their procurement budget is most vulnerable. <

Airline Safety Ground Ops and Audit Engine

April 2026

Designed and deployed a complete ETL data pipeline to audit 100,000+ commercial airline ground operations. Built with PostgreSQL, R, and Tableau, this project solves a critical aviation problem by transitioning from passive data dashboards to an active algorithmic alerting system. The engine automatically enforces strict SQL data quality gates, calculates geospatial safety risks, and triggers automated alerts when airports breach a 2.5% safety failure threshold. The accompanying Tableau dashboard visualizes the "Speed-Safety Paradox," proving that incentivizing faster aircraft turnarounds inadvertently increases catastrophic risk.

Predictive Analytics for Telecom Retention

March 2026
